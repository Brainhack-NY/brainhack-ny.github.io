Brainhack is a community-organized hackathon and unconference that brings together researchers and technologists from a myriad of disciplines to work together on innovative neuroscience projects. Since the first Brainhack in 2012, these events have been held in 8 countries on three continents and have become a model for open collaboration in science.

Following the success of previous distributed events (see Brainhack EDT and Brainhack Americas), Brainhack Global 2017 will take place simultaneously March 2â€“5 at 40 regional sites in 16 countries, allowing participants around the world to collaborate and share knowledge in real time despite being thousands of miles apart.

Please join us March 3-5 (Friday night, Saturday and Sunday) at Manylabs to hack, analyze, build, learn, ask questions, and make new friends.
